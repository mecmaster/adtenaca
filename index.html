Let's talk Proximity Market

Adtena is SaaS platform for Advertising and Marketing via Wi-Fi networks which integrate existing hotspots to single structure. We have beneficial user interface for our clients where they can manage indoor and outdoor Wi-Fi broadcasting and/or tracking hardware of our network. Our platform allows them to start and manage ads campaigns targeted by hyperlocal position and interests of audience. Using analytics provided by our platform you can better understand user experience and waypoints for marketing purposes (planning infrastructure, merchandising and strict ad campaigns, etc. ).
